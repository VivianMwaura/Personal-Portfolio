Delicious Fries Recipe

Learn how to make crispy, golden fries at home!

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes (Russet or Yukon Gold)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Optional: Pepper, garlic powder, or your favorite seasonings

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes (if desired) and cut them into fries. Aim for even thickness for uniform cooking.
  2. Soak the fries in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch (this helps them become crispier).
  3. Drain the fries and pat them dry with a clean towel.
  4. In a deep pot or deep-fryer, heat vegetable oil to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Carefully add the fries to the hot oil in batches to avoid overcrowding. Fry until golden brown, about 4-5 minutes.
  6. Using a slotted spoon, remove the fries and drain them on paper towels.
  7. While still hot, season with salt and any additional seasonings you like.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

Tips

  • For extra crispy fries, double fry them: fry at a lower temperature first, let them cool, and then fry again at a higher temperature.
  • Experiment with different seasonings like paprika, cayenne, or parmesan cheese.
  • For a healthier alternative, you can bake the fries in the oven: toss with a little oil and bake at 425°F (220°C) until golden.
